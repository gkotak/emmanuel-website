Foundations

Full specimens live in the Design System tab. The essentials:

Colour
Ecclesiastical navy, warm brown, tan/gold highlights, over aged-paper neutrals.
Type
EB Garamond + Cormorant + Manrope. Italic emphasis, wide-tracked eyebrows.
Shape
Square by default. Hairline rules divide; elevation is rare and neutral; motion is restrained.
