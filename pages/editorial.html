No. 01 · The Pattern of Prayer

A week in the life of Emmanuel.

The rhythm of our shared life is the regular celebration of the Eucharist — six weekly liturgies, each with its own voice. From Monday's contemplative quiet to the lively Joyful Noise on Sunday morning, all are welcome at our table.

i.
Sunday 8:00am

Said Eucharist, BCP

A short, 30-minute service offering a quiet, reflective start to the week, said according to the Book of Common Prayer.

ii.
Sunday 9:15am

Joyful Noise Eucharist

A lively celebration of the Mass for pre-school children and their families. Harin leads our music on the guitar. Not in school holidays.

iii.
Sunday 10:30am

Sung Eucharist

Our traditional celebration with music led by the parish choir; Children's & Youth Church meet in term-time, with All Age & Forest Church monthly.

iv.
Monday 14:30

School Collective Worship

Emmanuel School joins us for church once a week. On the first Monday of the month we celebrate the Eucharist together.

v.
Monday 18:30

Contemplative Eucharist

A simple, quiet service with a guided meditation and time for contemplative prayer to close the day.

vi.
Wednesday 12:30

Healing Eucharist

A said Eucharist with a focus on prayers of healing — for ourselves, the world, and those commended to our prayers. Anointing is offered.

No. 02 · A Warm Welcome

Wherever you find yourself, you will find a welcome here.

Emmanuel is a diverse, inclusive church in the liberal catholic tradition — a vibrant part of North London. Together, we are learning to be a community of faith shaped by the celebration of the Eucharist, a regular pattern of prayer and a vocation to share the all-encompassing love of God with the parish we serve.

Wherever you find yourself on your journey of exploration of the Christian faith — curious, doubtful, seeking, ambivalent or long-time devout believer — we trust you will find a warm welcome at Emmanuel.
