Admissions
By the church route

We welcome the whole school to the church on a weekly basis, where the Eucharist is celebrated on the first Monday of the month.

At weekly Collective Worship we make a point of introducing the pupils to the liturgy and doctrines that shape our faith, so that church will be a place that feels familiar to them for the rest of their lives. Our services include lively music, readings and prayers led by the pupils, and a strong emphasis on fostering curiosity and wonder.

Applying through the church route

If you are considering applying for a place at Emmanuel School for your child, please make very sure that you familiarise yourself with the admissions criteria.

To apply via the church route, you and your child will need to attend a Eucharistic service six times a quarter for the eight successive quarters prior to the date of application. In other words: if you want a place for September 2028, you will apply in January 2028 and will need to have been attending twice a month since January 2026.

If you have any questions regarding the church side of the admissions process, or about registering your child's attendance at church, please speak to the vicar.

Link to the school website and to Emmanuel School's admissions policy to follow.
