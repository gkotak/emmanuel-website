Service & Prayer Times

The Eucharist and the daily office shape the rhythm of our shared life. All are welcome at every service.

Sundays
8:00 · 9:15 · 10:30
Weekdays
Morning Prayer 9:00
Where
11 Lyncroft Gardens, NW6

As we gather to meet with Christ and one another, we seek to be a place where we are supported on our spiritual journeys, inspired to connect faith with the rest of our lives, and encouraged to put that faith into action.

The Eucharist sits at the heart of our understanding of who we are at Emmanuel. From the very young to the very old, we seek to equip our community to discover more fully the presence of God in their lives. To help us each find our place at the table, we have a number of services of Holy Communion during the week and on Sundays.

New to Emmanuel?

Not sure which service is right for you? We'd love to help you find your place.

What to expect →

Sundays

Three Eucharists
Sunday
8:00
Said Eucharist (BCP)
Said according to the Book of Common Prayer. This short, 30-minute service offers a quiet, reflective start to the week.
Sunday
9:15
Joyful Noise Eucharist
A lively celebration of the Mass designed for pre-school children and their families. Harin leads our music on the guitar and brings wonderful joy and energy to this service! Joyful Noise does not take place during the school summer holidays.
Sunday
10:30
Sung Eucharist
Our more traditional celebration of the Eucharist with music led by our parish choir. During term time our Children's Church and Youth Church meet during the service. On the first Sunday of the month the whole congregation gathers for All Age Worship, and on the third Sunday our children and youth meet for Forest Church in the garden.

In the week

Prayer & weekday Eucharists
Mon–Thu
9:00
Morning Prayer
A 20-minute service of prayer and Bible readings to start the day. All are welcome to join in person.
Monday
14:30
School Collective Worship
Emmanuel School join us for church once a week. On the first Monday of the month we celebrate the Eucharist together.
Monday
18:30
Contemplative Eucharist
A simple, quiet service with plenty of time for silence and contemplative prayer. All are welcome to join for Silent Prayer from 6:15pm.
Wednesday
12:30
Healing Eucharist
A said Eucharist with a focus on prayers of healing: for ourselves, the world, and those who have commended themselves to our prayers. Healing prayers and anointing are offered at this service.

All are welcome at every service. If you'd like to know more before visiting, do contact a member of the clergy — or simply come along.

Join us in prayer.

Whether for the first time or the thousandth, there is a place for you at the table at Emmanuel.
