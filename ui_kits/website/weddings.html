Weddings & Banns of Marriage

We love weddings — and are always delighted to pray for and accompany couples as they take this important step.

Where
Emmanuel or St Cuthbert’s
Preparation
3–4 sessions
First step
Contact the parish office

If you are engaged and considering getting married at Emmanuel or St Cuthbert's, we would love to hear from you!

We love weddings and are always delighted to pray for and accompany couples as they take this important step. We warmly encourage couples to attend three or four marriage preparation sessions ahead of the wedding day; these will be arranged between the priest and the couple, and will ensure that sessions are compatible with work and other commitments.

If you would like to know more about weddings in either of our churches, please contact the parish office in the first instance and a member of the clergy will be in touch.

A common question

Can I get married at Emmanuel?

If you live in the parish, it is very straightforward. If you don't, we need to establish whether you have a qualifying connection — you only need one of the following:

  • You and/or your fiancé(e) are on the electoral roll of Emmanuel or St Cuthbert's.
  • One or both of you were baptised in the parish (by a Church of England service).
  • One or both of you have been confirmed (by a Church of England service), with the confirmation entered in a register belonging to a church in the parish.
  • One or both of you have parents or grandparents who were married in the parish by a Church of England service.
  • One or both of you have had your usual place of residence in the parish for at least 6 months.
  • One or both of your parents had their usual place of residence in the parish for at least 6 months during your lifetime.
  • One or both of you have habitually attended public worship at Church of England services in the parish for at least 6 months.
  • One or both of your parents have habitually attended public worship at Church of England services in the parish for at least 6 months during your lifetime.
