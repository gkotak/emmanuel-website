Community Outreach

A vocation to share the all-encompassing love of God with the parish we serve — through practical action alongside our neighbours.

West Hampstead Community Food Hub

We have a collection point for the West Hampstead Community Food Hub in both entrances to the church.

Food is collected on Fridays and contributes to the WH Foodhub, which runs out of Sidings Community Centre on Saturday mornings. If you would like to support the Foodhub financially or with your time, do get in touch with them directly.
