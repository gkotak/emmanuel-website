The Electoral Roll

If you worship with us regularly, joining the electoral roll is a simple way to say: Emmanuel is my church.

The electoral roll is how we keep track of how many people think of themselves as members of Emmanuel Church.

It also helps the Diocese calculate the amount of money we pay towards central church costs. To elect new members to the Parochial Church Council or the Deanery Synod, you need to be on the electoral roll.

If you worship with us on a regular basis, please consider joining the Electoral Roll — we would be glad to count you among our members.
