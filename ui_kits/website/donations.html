How can I help?

It costs £15,000 a month to run Emmanuel. We raise all of it ourselves, locally — it doesn’t come from the Government or the Church of England.

Monthly running cost
£15,000
Funded by
Our congregation
Gift Aid adds
+25%

While we hold fundraising events and rent out the church, one of the foundations of our finances is the tremendous support we receive from our congregation through regular donations.

That £15,000 a month covers everything it takes to keep Emmanuel open, alive and serving the parish — from the ministry of our clergy to the upkeep of a Victorian building, and the outreach that reaches beyond our walls.

Where it goes

What your giving supports

Ministry

Clergy pay, housing and pension — the people who lead our worship and care.

Building

Maintenance, utilities and insurance for a much-loved Victorian church.

Administration

Office costs for the day-to-day operation of the parish and its activities.

Services

The direct costs of worship — music, liturgy, candles, vestments and more.

Outreach

Reaching beyond our walls — the food bank, night shelter and other care.

The simplest way

How to donate

We recommend the Parish Giving Scheme portal. It reduces our administration, improves our budgeting process, and helps us collect Gift Aid — an additional 25% on top of every eligible gift, at no extra cost to you.

Setting up a regular gift through the scheme takes only a few minutes, and you stay in full control — you can change or stop your giving whenever you wish.

Give via the Parish Giving Scheme
+25%
Gift Aid adds 25p to every £1 you give, reclaimed from HMRC — if you’re a UK taxpayer, just tick the box.

Why a regular gift helps most

Predictable monthly giving lets us plan ahead with confidence, rather than relying on one-off donations and events alone.

A gentle guide

How much to donate

This is clearly personal — but the following may help you decide what feels right for you.

3%

The historic guidance from the Church of England is to consider giving 3% of your disposable income — that is, income after tax and household bills.

☕

Some find it easier to compare their giving to other everyday costs — a few coffees, or a gym membership — and give accordingly.

?

If you have any questions, contact Geoffrey Shaw, our Parish Giving Officer, at emmanuelchurchgiving@gmail.com.

Beyond giving

Other ways you can contribute

There are many ways to get involved and help Emmanuel — through our services, the upkeep of the building, and supporting the wider community. Here are just a few ideas.

Reading at a service

Share in the liturgy by reading the lessons aloud.

Joining the choir

Add your voice to the music of the Sung Eucharist.

Greeting people

Welcome visitors and regulars as they arrive at church.

Sunday School

Lead or support our children during the morning service.

Gardening

Help keep the church grounds and green looking their best.

Small building works

Lend a hand with the practical upkeep of the building.

If you’d like to help us, please talk to one of the clergy or Church Wardens, or email us at info@emmanuelnw6.com.

Every gift keeps the doors open.

Thank you for supporting the life, worship and ministry of Emmanuel Church.
